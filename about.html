Jaid Stairmand

I’m a self-employed hairstylist based at The Hair Co. Tauranga. Hair is my passion! I love making people glow from the inside out by simply making their hair look great! Creating a style that suits your day to day routine is what I like to achieve for my clients! Whether you need something you can wash and wear, or if you prefer to use hot tools or styling products. I offer a range of services from fashion colors, to cuts, styling and lash and brow tints. I started in the industry working at a salon after school and on weekends when I was 12. That's when I discovered my passion for hairdressing. I went on to do my apprenticeship 4 years later in 2009 and qualified in 2012.
